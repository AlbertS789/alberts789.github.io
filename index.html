About

Some Words About Me.

The world of data is one of the most important and it is indeed the field that I've most interest in since more than 5 years. I love to learn and to collaborate with people to create different products and to discuss new ideas, moreover when it is related to ML. Also, teaching is another field that I am interested in. That is why sometimes I like to write about different topics but mainly related to Data Science. I believe that every single data is important as long as you know how to use it. In addition, I like to read and learn about Machine Learning and AI, so then I can put my knowledge in practice to create value to the projects I've worked on.

Data Science

The modern world is full of data, every single second data is produced in many ways. Working and give meaning to all that data is a special characterist of Data Science. From Data Engineering to Machine Learning, Data Science topics are in every single step of the pipeline, and each part of this is important for companies nowadays. I have enjoyed for more than 4 years to work with data, finding insights, training models and present them in a meaningful way for business.

Teaching

Helping to understands different topics that I interested in is a another passion of mine. Therefore, teaching is a field that matches perfectly that aspect of my life. I have worked for more than 5 years in the teaching field, recently focusing on lecturing about topics related with AI and Machine Learning to undergrads and giving conferences. All the material that I have created online is just another tool, from the many I use, to help students to understand better the magnificent world of data.

Writing

The Data Science universe is huge, and it is expanding everyday. So, I believe that although there are many bloggers out there, just a few can explain what data science and all fields used on it are really about. Having this thought in mind, I dedicate part of my free time to write on my Medium Blog called Adquisition of Learning (AL). There I write about different topics related to data, from basic stuff like Python to explaining more ellaborate topics like Reinforcement Learning.

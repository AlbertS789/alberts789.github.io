About

Some Words About Me.

As a passionate data enthusiast with over five years of experience, I am committed to exploring the endless possibilities that the world of data offers. Every piece of data is valuable, and its true potential is unlocked when we know how to leverage it effectively. I believe that data is one of the most powerful tools at our disposal, and I continuously seek to expand my knowledge and stay updated with the latest developments in Machine Learning and AI. Applying this knowledge to create innovative solutions and bring value to the projects I undertake is my primary goal. Additionally, sharing my knowledge and teaching others about Data Science is an area of interest, and I frequently write on various related topics. Let's explore the exciting world of data together, unlock its true potential, and create something exceptional.

Data Science

In today's fast-paced world, data is being produced every second in various forms. As a Data Science professional, I have developed the ability to work with this vast amount of data and extract meaningful insights from it. Whether it's Data Engineering or Machine Learning, the different aspects of the Data Science pipeline are crucial for companies to thrive in today's competitive market. For over 5 years, I have had the pleasure of working with data, from uncovering hidden patterns and training models to presenting findings that are both insightful and relevant to business objectives. I find it extremely fulfilling to be part of this dynamic field and to use my skills to make a difference.

Teaching

As an avid learner, I find great satisfaction in sharing my knowledge and helping others understand complex topics that I'm interested in. Teaching is a passion that perfectly aligns with this aspect of my life. With over five years of experience in the field, I have focused primarily on lecturing about AI and Machine Learning to undergraduates, as well as giving conferences on the topic. All the online material I have created is just one of the many tools I use to help students gain a deeper understanding of the fascinating world of data. It brings me immense joy to see my students grow and succeed, knowing that I played a part in their journey.

Writing

The field of Data Science and AI is vast and ever-expanding. Despite the multitude of bloggers out there, few can truly capture the essence of what Data Science and AI are all about. That's why I dedicate part of my free time to writing on my Medium Blog called "Acuity Learning" (AL). My aim is to provide readers with a comprehensive understanding of various data-related topics, ranging from the fundamentals of Python to the particulars of Reinforcement Learning. Through my writing, I hope to demystify the world of data and inspire others to explore this exciting field. With a deep passion for learning and an insatiable curiosity, I take great pride in sharing my knowledge and expertise with others.

Reinforcement Learning to Reduce the Electricity Consumption and Billing on the Demand Side

Model using decentralized multi-agent reinforcement learning to take decisions regarding the usage of electricity. Modeling the appliances considering their purpose and frequency of use, mainly focusing on those for cooking and cleaning. Giving individual suggestion of the turning on or off those appliances. Also, considering the customer into the system and modeling the decisions as a fuzzy system.
